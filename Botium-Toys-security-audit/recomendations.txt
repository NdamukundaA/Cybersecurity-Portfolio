# Recommendations for Botium Toys Security Improvements

1. **Implement Least Privilege**:
   - Restrict employee access to sensitive data based on job roles and responsibilities.
   - Ensure that only authorized personnel have access to credit card and PII data.

2. **Use Encryption for Sensitive Data**:
   - Apply encryption protocols (e.g., AES-256) for all sensitive customer information, both at rest and in transit.
   - Encrypt credit card data as it is processed, stored, and transmitted.

3. **Establish a Disaster Recovery Plan**:
   - Create a comprehensive disaster recovery and business continuity plan.
   - Ensure regular backups of critical data are stored securely off-site.

4. **Install an Intrusion Detection System (IDS)**:
   - Implement an IDS to monitor the internal network for suspicious activity.
   - Regularly update IDS rules to detect emerging threats.

5. **Improve Password Policy**:
   - Update the password policy to require a minimum of 8 characters, including upper and lower case letters, numbers, and special characters.
   - Enforce the use of a password management system to centralize and control password policies.

6. **Implement Regular Audits**:
   - Conduct regular security audits to ensure compliance with PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC Type 1 and 2 standards.
   - Review and update security controls periodically to address new threats.
